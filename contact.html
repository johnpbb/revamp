Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about connecting to the network.

How fast can we get started?

Onboarding typically takes 48-72 hours. Once your account is verified, you can start loading inventory or booking immediately.

Do you support regional currencies?

Yes, we support FJD, AUD, NZD, USD, TOP, and VUV with real-time exchange rate synchronization via our API integrations.

Is there a setup fee?

We have flexible plans ranging from zero-setup commission-based models to enterprise fixed-fee solutions tailored to your operational size.

Can I integrate my existing PMS?

Absolutely. We have a robust REST/XML API and pre-built connectors for most major Property Management Systems used in the Pacific.
